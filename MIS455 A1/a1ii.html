List of some private University

IUB

Independent University, Bangladeso (IUB) is a private university in Bangladesh. It is located in Bashundhara Residential Area of Dhaka, Bangladesh. It was established in 1993. IUB has a website called IRAS. In every semester IRAS play game with the student of IUB.

BRAC

BRAC University is a private research university located in Dhaka, Bangladesh. It is also known as BracU It was established in 2001 as a branch of Sir Fazle Hasan Abed's BRAC under the Private University Act 1992.

NSU

North South University is a private university based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. It is also known as NSU. Its business school is the first Bangladeshi university to receive American accreditation from the ACBSP in 2015.
